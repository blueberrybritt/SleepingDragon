About Us

Who are we?

The idea of the Sleeping Dragon originally came from a couple named Grant and Britt. They love to play board games and wanted to create a cozy environment where people could come together and play games. One had a large knowledge of games, and the other had a love of baking so naturally it just came together and made sense.

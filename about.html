About Us

What is the Sleeping Dragon?

The Sleeping Dragon is a cafe designed to bring fun to people of all ages. We want to bring the joy of board games to our community in a cozy environment. With our expansive library of games and our delicious menu, it's a pretty hard opportunity to pass up!

How Does it Work?

At the Sleeping Dragon one of our key features is playing board games! How do you get to enjoy these games you may ask? It's quite simple, $5 per hour per person for access to any games in our extensive library. And while you are playing these games you can order some snacks from our menu to keep yourself fueled!

Who are We?

Grant and Britt are a recently engaged couple hailing from Louisiana. What bonded them together when they first started dating was board games! They love to play board games and wanted to share their love with everyone else in the form of the Sleeping Dragon. With Grant's extensive board game knowledge and Britt's love of baking, the combination just made sense.

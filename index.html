Don't Sleep on the Sleeping Dragon!

The Sleeping Dragon is Downtown's very first Board Game Cafe! It's fun for everyone! There's a game to suit any interest or skill level! For only $5 per hour per person, you can play any of our games from our extensive library. While you're playing games feel free to order delectible pastries and snacks from our menu!
